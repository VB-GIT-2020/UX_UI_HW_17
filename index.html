Hi there! I am Vibha Bhagwat. I am a UX Researcher and Designer. A business and environment-focused design thinker and problem-solver, I strive to collaboratively strike the perfect balance between customer needs and corporate goals

Designing for Difference

“Don’t find customers for your products, find products for your customers”
- Seth Godin


Vibha Bhagwat is a Behavioral Researcher - Business Manager - Banker - Space Designer - Lifelong Design Thinker, who finally found the passion of her life in UX Research & Design, because of the amazing power it has in bringing betterment for lives of customers and the bottomline of businesses.

Vibha has a background in Psychology and Marketing and nearly 20 years of experience in different industries in the DC Metro area. Vibha is a strong advocate for enabling meaningful designs and reducing wastefulness from thoughtlessly created products.

Her exceptional skills, tools, talents, creative hobbies and global travel & cultural experiences have all fed into her passion for creative problem-solving with a focus on empathetic research and business solutions.

Vibha looks forward to connecting with you to take on product and service usability challenges to make for meaningful designs for your customers and make a difference to the business bottomline. Let’s talk!
