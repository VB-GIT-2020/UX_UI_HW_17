Her exceptional skills, tools, talents, creative hobbies and global travel & cultural experiences have all fed into her passion for creative problem-solving with a focus on empathetic research and business solutions.

Vibha looks forward to connecting with you to take on product and service usability challenges to make for meaningful designs for your customers and make a difference to the business bottomline.
